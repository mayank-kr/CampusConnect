ABOUT

Welcome to Campus Connect - the ultimate app designed to make campus life easier for students! With a range of features including buy and sell, lost and found, academic calendar, mess menu, time table, cab sharing, news and updates, and important contacts, Campus Connect is the go-to app for all your campus needs.

Buy and Sell

Our buy and sell feature allows students to buy and sell textbooks, electronics, and other items with ease. With a secure platform and easy-to-use interface, students can connect with each other.

Lost and Found

The lost and found feature helps students to report and search for lost items. Whether it's a misplaced textbook or a lost wallet, Campus Connect makes it easy for students to connect with each other and reunite lost items with their owners.

Academic Calender

The academic calendar feature keeps students up-to-date with all their upcoming exams, classes, and events. Students can plan their schedule ahead of time and stay on top of their academic commitments.

Mess Menu

The mess menu feature provides students with the daily menu of the campus mess, making it easy to plan their meals in advance. With a simple and easy-to-use interface, students can view the menu and decide what they want to eat for the day.

Time Table

The time table feature helps students to keep track of their classes and manage their time effectively. With customizable schedules, students can add and edit their classes, set reminders, and manage their daily routine efficiently.

Cab Sharing

The cab sharing feature allows students to connect with each other and share rides to and from campus. With a secure platform and easy-to-use interface, students can find and connect with other students who are traveling in the same direction, saving money and reducing their carbon footprint.

New and Updates

The news and updates feature keeps students up-to-date with all the latest campus news, events, and announcements. With a simple and easy-to-use interface, students can stay informed about all the important events happening on campus.

Important Contacts

The important contacts feature provides students with a list of all the important contacts they may need while on campus. From academic advisors to emergency contacts, Campus Connect makes it easy for students to find the right person to help them when they need it most.


At Campus Connect, we are committed to making campus life easier for students. With a range of features designed to meet the needs of students, we are the go-to app for all your campus needs. Download the app today and take control of your campus life!
